Engagement Overview

GSTV x Prosper and Partners GTM strategy and narrative refresh. GSTV recently acquired by MidOcean Partners (PE). Sales declining after 5–6 years of strong growth. Core problem: narrative-market fit failure, not product-market fit failure.

Core Problem

  • Historical CTV platform narrative no longer sufficient to win
  • CTV supply glut driving pricing pressure across the category
  • Agency buying behavior shifting to outcome-based compensation
  • Retail media and attention metrics rising in importance
  • GSTV excluded from planning conversations — narrative is the unlock

Strategic Direction

  • Reframe: from "TV at the pump" to national video platform, retail media amplifier, outcome-driven channel
  • Lead with incremental reach, high-attention environment and retail impact
  • Category-specific narratives: QSR, Retail, CPG, Financial Services, Auto
  • Build a sales enablement function — currently absent
  • Retail media partnerships: CVS Media, Walmart Connect, Roundel

Key Stakeholders

  • GSTV leadership: Scott and Rob (executive sponsors)
  • Prosper and Partners: embedded, hands-on operating model
  • GSTV marketing and sales teams: day-to-day collaborators

Open Questions
